### 5. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

#### 5.1 Where your data is stored

Your data is stored on servers operated by DigitalOcean in their Singapore region (the closest available region to Bangladesh with appropriate data centre certifications). Certain data may be cached or processed through Cloudflare's global edge network.

We are committed to bringing data storage closer to Bangladesh as infrastructure options improve.

#### 5.2 Security measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS 1.3 for all connections)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive fields (payment credentials, API tokens encrypted with AES-256-GCM)
- Password hashing using bcrypt with cost factor 12
- JWT authentication with RS256 asymmetric signing
- Multi-tenant row-level database isolation (PostgreSQL RLS)
- Regular security testing and penetration testing
- Access controls limiting staff access to minimum necessary data
- Two-factor authentication required for all administrative accounts

No security system is impenetrable. In the event of a data breach that poses a significant risk to users, we will notify affected users within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, and report to relevant authorities as required by Bangladesh law.

#### 5.3 Data retention

We retain your personal information for as long as:
- Your account is active, plus a reasonable period after account closure
- Required by law (financial records: 7 years per Bangladesh tax and commerce law)
- Necessary for legitimate business purposes

Specific retention periods:

| Data Category | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Account information | 3 years after account closure |

### 4. THE MARKETPLACE — LISTING RULES

#### 4.1 Dealer Listings

Dealer vehicle listings are automatically published to the marketplace when a vehicle is added with minimum required information and ≥ 4 photos. By publishing a listing, you represent and warrant that:

- The vehicle exists and is in your possession or under your legal control
- All information in the listing is accurate and not misleading
- The asking price is your genuine asking price (not artificially inflated for the purpose of appearing discounted)
- You have the right to sell the vehicle
- The vehicle is free of any liens or encumbrances that would prevent transfer of title, or any such encumbrances are disclosed in the listing

#### 4.2 C2C Listing Rules

C2C Listings are subject to moderation before going live. By submitting a C2C listing, you represent and warrant the same as Section 4.1 above. In addition:

- You may only list vehicles that you personally own
- Dealers using a personal account to list business inventory are prohibited
- Listings must include genuine photos of the actual vehicle being sold (not stock photos or photos from another listing)
- Contact information displayed must be your own and valid

#### 4.3 Prohibited Listings

The following are strictly prohibited on the AutoVerse marketplace:

- Vehicles with cloned, altered, or fraudulent registration documents
- Stolen vehicles
- Vehicles with tampered odometer readings presented as accurate
- Duplicate listings of the same vehicle
- Listings where the seller has no right to sell
- Vehicles that do not meet Bangladesh road safety or emission standards (where required for registration)
- Listings containing false or materially misleading information
- Listings with prices set solely to deceive buyers about market value

AutoVerse reserves the right to remove any listing, without notice or refund, that violates these rules or that AutoVerse, in its sole discretion, considers inappropriate.

#### 4.4 Moderation

All C2C listings are reviewed by AutoVerse content moderators before going live. Moderation outcomes:
- **Approved:** Listing goes live; seller notified
- **Rejected:** Listing not published; seller notified with reason; seller may resubmit up to 3 times
- **Flagged:** Listing published but monitored; seller notified if action required

Dealer listings are published automatically but may be subject to post-publication review upon buyer reports. If 2 or more unique buyers report a listing within 24 hours, the listing is temporarily hidden pending moderator review.

#### 4.5 IMV Ratings

The IMV deal rating (Great Deal / Good Deal / Fair Price / Above Market / No Rating) is an automated statistical estimate based on comparable active listings in the same market cluster. It is provided for informational purposes only and does not represent a valuation, appraisal, warranty, or guarantee of any vehicle's condition, value, or suitability. AutoVerse is not responsible for any buying or selling decisions made based on IMV ratings.

IMV ratings require a minimum sample size of 10 comparable listings to be displayed. Below this threshold, "No Rating" is shown. Listings with "No Rating" should not be assumed to be overpriced or underpriced.
